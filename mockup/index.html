Better Late than Never, I Suppose

Published May 22, 2008

Well, I suppose it's time. I've avoided jumping online with an official-like presence for long enough. At this point I feel like the guy in the 90's who was still using a Walkman listening to cassette tapes.

What will you find here? Mostly a discussion on software development, software teams, and other related technical bits and pieces. You'll find a healthy dose of cynicism and pragmatism in my viewpoints, but more importantly I hope there is a fair bit of humor.

ABOUT

Hi, I'm Louis Marascio. I'm an entrepreneur and software developer in Austin, TX. I enjoy writing code and building companies that solve interesting problems. Welcome.

WORK

I'm currently employed by Cisco Systems where I am responsible for engineering the Cisco Unified Application Environment, the development platform for the Cisco Unified Communications product line.

METREOS

I was a co-founder and the CTO of Metreos, a software company in Austin, TX. In June 2006 Metreos was acquired by Cisco Systems, where I currently work. Metreos was a big part of my life and I've decided to archive as many of the bits of wisdom and funny stories that flew by so quickly while we built our company that I can remember. When I have time to start remembering some of the more interesting bits I'll update this section.
